IMMORTALIZED JOURNEY

Editorial, Graphic Design (August 2017 - May 2017)

Matigda, is the official yearbook publication of Bukidnon State University. I was tasked to create content, posters, and the yearbook for the graduating students and I've always been a champion for print media.

The challenge was to build a yearbook that is modern but timeless. A lot has changed for the yearbook. Fortunately, the 2017 Yearbook was released before graduation and the editorial board is thankful for the positive reception.
